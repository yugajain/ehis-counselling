The
Career Counselling
Cell

The Career Counselling Cell at Emerald Heights International School is dedicated to guiding and empowering our students on their educational and professional journeys. Comprising experienced and qualified counsellors, our cell provides comprehensive career guidance, assisting students in making informed choices about their future paths. From helping them explore their interests and strengths to offering insights into various academic and vocational options, we strive to nurture well-rounded individuals equipped for success. Our goal is to ensure that every student at Emerald Heights is well-prepared to embark on a fulfilling and meaningful career, aligned with their aspirations and potential.

Explore how we train various classes

Class 8

In class 8 at Emerald Heights International School, we place a strong emphasis on collaborative learning and skill development through group sessions. Our students engage in a variety of group activities and projects that encourage teamwork, communication, and problem-solving. These sessions not only enhance their academic knowledge but also foster critical life skills, such as leadership, time management, and adaptability. By working together on assignments and projects, students learn to appreciate diverse perspectives and leverage their collective strengths. Our aim is to prepare them not just for academic success but also for the challenges of the real world, where effective collaboration and well-honed skills are essential for personal and professional growth.

Class 9

In class 9 at Emerald Heights International School, our commitment to fostering holistic development continues with frequent group sessions that play a pivotal role in skill enhancement and career preparation. These sessions are designed to provide students with hands-on experiences, encouraging them to apply their academic knowledge in practical contexts. Through collaborative projects and discussions, students refine their critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills. Moreover, these group activities also introduce them to various career pathways, helping them make more informed decisions about their future. By the end of class 9, our students not only possess a solid academic foundation but also a repertoire of essential skills and insights that will serve as a strong foundation for their upcoming educational journey and the career opportunities that lie ahead.

Class 10

In class 10 at Emerald Heights International School, we focus on profile building and nurturing well-rounded individuals. While academic excellence remains a core goal, we also emphasize the importance of co-curricular activities and personal development in shaping a comprehensive profile. Our students are encouraged to actively participate in a wide array of extracurricular pursuits, from sports and arts to community service and leadership roles within the school. These experiences not only enhance their skills but also help them develop a strong sense of responsibility, teamwork, and leadership. Our aim is to empower our students to craft profiles that reflect not just their academic achievements but also their unique talents, interests, and contributions, ensuring they are well-prepared for future educational pursuits and professional endeavors.

Class 11

In class 11 at Emerald Heights International School, we prioritize a student-centric approach to subject selection and academic growth. We believe that a crucial step towards a successful educational journey is choosing subjects aligned with a student's interests and future aspirations. Our dedicated counseling and academic advisory teams work closely with students to provide guidance in selecting the most suitable subjects. Once subjects are chosen, we offer specialized sessions and resources to ensure students have a strong foundation in each area. These sessions are designed to deepen their understanding, improve their skills, and foster a genuine passion for their chosen subjects. By nurturing their academic interests and facilitating comprehensive subject-specific support, we empower our class 11 students to excel academically and develop the knowledge and skills needed for their future endeavors.

Class 12

In class 12 at Emerald Heights International School, we place a significant emphasis on guiding students through the college application process and helping them make informed decisions about their higher education journey. Our dedicated college counseling team offers personalized support, assisting students in researching and selecting colleges or universities that align with their academic goals and career aspirations. We organize university fairs and college fairs to expose our students to a wide range of higher education options, allowing them to interact with representatives from various institutions and gather valuable information. Furthermore, we provide comprehensive guidance on the application process, including assistance with essays, interviews, and standardized tests. Our ultimate aim is to empower class 12 students to choose the right educational path and successfully navigate the college admissions process, setting them on a promising trajectory for their future.
